## 🎯 What are we looking for?
We don't want just cold resumes. We want to know about:
* **The Spark:** What made you type your first `Hello World`?
* **The "Valley of Death":** Which technical error or challenge almost made you quit?
* **The Toolbox:** What are you studying today and what do you recommend for those a step behind you (courses, books, channels)?

✍️ How to leave your mark (Step by Step)
Contributing is as simple as a git commit:

Fork this repository.

Copy the content from the stories/template.md file.

Create a new file in stories/your-name-surname.md and paste the content.

Write your journey! Feel free to add links to courses, books, and videos that changed your game.

Send a Pull Request.

💡 Tips for a great story
Be Sincere: Stories of overcoming obstacles inspire much more than stories of absolute perfection.

Share Resources: If that Java course or that Clean Code video helped you, leave the link to make life easier for those reading!

Networking: At the end of your file, feel free to leave your LinkedIn or personal website.

📜 Code of Conduct
Respect above all. This is an environment for collective mentoring, mutual support, and diversity. Offensive or discriminatory comments will not be accepted in Pull Requests.

Why participate?
Many beginners feel lost with the information overload (the famous analysis paralysis). Your tip about a specific course or your story of how you landed your first job might be exactly the compass someone needs today.
